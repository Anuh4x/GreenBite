Avocado Toast

Wholegrain toast topped with smashed avocado, lemon and chili flakes.

Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices wholegrain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Chili flakes
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado with lemon, salt and pepper.
  3. Spread on toast and sprinkle chili flakes.

Quinoa Salad Bowl

Colorful bowl with quinoa, chickpeas, cucumber, tomato and tahini.

Lunch

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Combine quinoa and veggies.
  2. Whisk tahini with lemon and a little water.
  3. Drizzle dressing and toss.

Berry Yogurt Parfait

Layers of Greek yogurt, berries and crunchy granola.

Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Layer yogurt, berries, and granola in a glass.
  2. Finish with a drizzle of honey.

Veggie Stir Fry

Mixed seasonal vegetables quickly sautéed with light soy sauce and garlic.

Dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, peppers)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or pan.
  2. Add garlic and vegetables; stir-fry 4–5 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and toss to coat. Serve warm.

Fruit Smoothie

Refreshing smoothie with banana, berries and yogurt.

Drink

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk or almond milk

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy.

Chickpea Curry

Hearty and protein-rich curry with chickpeas and tomatoes.

Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp curry powder

Steps

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in a little oil until soft.
  2. Add curry powder and cook 1 minute.
  3. Add chickpeas and tomatoes; simmer 15 minutes.
